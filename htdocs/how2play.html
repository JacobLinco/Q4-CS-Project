Instructions

General Instructions:

As the title suggests, this website is a trivia quiz on gaming! You will answer different types of items in the next page, and you will be ranked based on your scores. Some items may earn more points than others and some 'lifelines' (to be explained later) may also affect the pointes gained from a question.

Item Types:

  • True or False

    • Standard 2-choice item where a statement will be given and and the player answers if it is true or false


  • Multiple Choice

    • Standard multiple choice item where a question is asked and the player chooses which choice they think is right


  • Short Answer

    • Standard text-answer item where the player enters text based on what the item asks for


  • Sus 'Em Out

    • This item will have 4 game-related things which all have a single similarity except for one, which you have to 'sus out.' The hidden similarity will be revealed when the item is answered


  • Who's the Crewmate?

    • A similarity will be given along with some things that have that similarity. The player is given choices, and they have to choose the choice that is part of the 'set,' or the choie that is not sus


  • Listen!

    • In this item, you will have to listen to a certain audio - whether it be a sound effect or soundtrack - and you have to choose which game it is from.


  • Number Estimation

    • For this item, a question will be asked with the answer being a number. The player has to guess what this number, but there will be an accepted range so the guess does not have to be exact. Exact guesses will give more points.



    Lifelines:

  • 50/50

    • This lifeline will remove 2 incorrect choices. Only usable for multiple choices.


  • Hint

    • This lifeline will give you a hint! When hints are used, the question will only give half the points


  • 1st letter

    • This lifelinfe will give the 1st letter/number of an item. Only usable for short-text and estimation items.
